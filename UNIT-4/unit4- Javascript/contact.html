Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book a package?+
You can book directly on our website by clicking "Book Now" on any package page, or contact us via phone / WhatsApp. Our team will guide you through the process and confirm your booking within 24 hours.
What is your cancellation policy?+
We offer free cancellation up to 7–10 days before departure depending on the package. Cancellations made within 7 days may incur a 20–50% cancellation fee. Please check the specific package for exact terms.
Do you arrange visa assistance?+
Yes! We provide complete visa guidance and documentation support for all international packages. Visa fees themselves are not included in the package price, but our team will assist you with the entire process.
Can I customise a package?+
Absolutely! All our packages can be fully customised. Just contact our travel consultants with your preferences — destination, budget, duration, activities — and we'll craft a personalised itinerary for you.
Is travel insurance included?+
Travel insurance is included in our Family and Honeymoon packages. For the Adventure package, specialised adventure sports insurance is included. For standalone bookings, we offer optional travel insurance at an additional cost.
