Sample Itinerary

1
Day 1
Arrival & Gear Up 🎒
Airport pickup. Hotel check-in. Full safety briefing. Gear distribution and fitting. Evening team-building bonfire and route planning session.
2
Day 2
Trek & Paragliding 🪂
Morning acclimatization hike (6–8 km). Afternoon tandem paragliding over scenic valleys. Evening at base camp with mountain views.
3
Day 3
Water Adventures 🌊
White water rafting on grade 3–4 rapids. Cliff jumping at natural pools. Kayaking at sunset. Campfire dinner by the river.
4

Two-day summit trek with certified guides. Optional rock climbing and scuba diving on day 5. Certificate of completion awarded!
5

Victory breakfast. Photo memories and certificate ceremony. Airport drop. You're now officially an adventurer!
